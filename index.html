01.
Wonders of Winter

Where: Waterloo Park
When: November 25, 2023 - January 1, 2024
Hours: 5:00PM-10:30PM
Website: https://www.wondersofwinter.ca/

Discover Waterloo Park in the dark this holiday season! Wonders of Winter is a Christmas themed lights experience held in Waterloo Park. Ideal for those of all ages. Check out the lights individually, as a family, or with a your group of friends. This event is the perfect way to get into the Christma spirit. The light displays featured consist of well known characters from children's tv shows such as Spongebob Squarepants and Olaf to your classic Christmas tree and Santa and his reindeer.

02.
Christkindl Market

Where: Downtown Kitchener
When: December 07, 2023 - December 10, 2023
Hours: 10:00 AM-8:00PM (*4:00PM on December 10)
Website: https://www.kitchener.ca/en/arts-culture-and-events/christkindl-market.aspx

The Christkrindl Market is a family-friendly four day Christmas event with a German take. Some of the things you can look forward to during your visit are live music performances, live ice sculpting, German delicacies, and indoor and outdoor shops.
